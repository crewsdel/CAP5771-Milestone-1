We designed a static dashboard notebook to clearly communicate our findings thus far in the project.
The goal was to make the results easy to explore and understand without overwhelming the viewer.

We included interactive widgets that allow the user to select a state, AQI metric, and model type.
This makes it possible to explore how patterns vary across different regions and conditions.
The dashboard includes time series plots, model prediction comparisons between Linear Regression and Random Forest, scatter plots, and feature importance charts.

We focused on keeping the visuals simple, well-labeled, and tied to our main question.
The dashboard helps demonstrate that AQI and recent air quality trends are meaningful predictors of respiratory admissions, and that the Random Forest model performs better than Linear Regression.
